About Mozilla

Mozilla is a free software community founded in 1998 by members of Netscape. Our mission is to ensure the Internet is a global public resource, open and accessible to all.

Our Products

Mozilla offers a wide range of products and services, including:

  • Firefox - A fast, private, and secure browser
  • Firefox Developer Edition - Tools for web developers
  • Firefox Focus - A privacy-focused browser for mobile
  • Mozilla VPN - Protect your online privacy
